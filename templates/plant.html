Rauwolfia serpentina

Local Name: sarpagandha

Type of plant: Shrub

Family: Apocynaceae

Aliments Treated: Hypertension, insomnia, fever, rheumatic fever, bronchitis, asthma, gastric ulcer and pain. The root juice is especially effective in snake venom and insect bites. Reserpine is obtained from the bark of the sarpagandha plant, which is used as a medicine to lower high blood pressure.

Boerhavia diffusa L.

Local Name: punarnava

Type of plant: Herb

Family: Nyctaginaceae

Aliments treated:Roots are employed for many purposes including liver, gallbladder, kidney, renal and urinary disorders. Leaves are useful in jaundice, anemia, ascites, ophthalmia and dropsy.

Datura Stramonium L.

Local Name: kalodhutura

Type of plant: Shrub

Family: Solanaceae

Aliments Treated: Alleviating pain, treating fever, enhancing heart functions, improving fertility, inducing sleep, easing childbirth and promoting hair and skin health.

Artocarpus chama

Local Name: chapalish

Type of plant: Tree

Family: Moraceae

Aliments Treated: Diarrhea, skin diseases, asthma, ulcers etc
